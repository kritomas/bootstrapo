Prerequisites

You're going to need a USB Drive (4GB+ should be enough), and the Linux distribution's ISO installation file. Burn the ISO to the USB via Rufus, Unetbootin, or something similar. Alternatively, you can use Ventoy, and just move it onto the USB.

Installation

Reboot your computer. Use BIOS or something to boot into the USB. Follow the installers instruction.

Welcome to Linux

Reboot again. Your computer should now boot into your brand new Linux System! Extra configuration may be necessary (varies system-to-system)
